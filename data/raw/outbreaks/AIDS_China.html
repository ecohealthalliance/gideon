Demography and risk factors:
- Cases reported to March 1998: 91% ages 13 to 49; 94% males; 17% heterosexual; 5% men who have sex with men; 59% IDU; 20% transfusion or hemophilia-related.
- Cases during 1996 to 1998: 98% ages 13 to 49; 83% males; 22% heterosexual; 0% men who have sex with men; 58% IDU; 18% transfusion-related; 1% mother-to-infant.
- Cases during 1998 to 2000: 87% males; 34% heterosexual; 1% men who have sex with men; 60% IDU; 2% transfusion-related; 1% mother-to-infant.
- Cases during 1997 to 2001: 91% ages 15 to 49; 78% males; 28% heterosexual; 1% men who have sex with men; 48% IDU; 6% transfusion/hemophilia; 1% mother to infant.

Students ages 15 to 24 accounted for 0.39% of HIV/AIDS cases in 2006, 0.50% in 2007, 0.83% in 2008 and 1.02% in 2009. 2

The true number of AIDS cases to December 1997 is estimated at 9,000, with 6,400 AIDS deaths.
