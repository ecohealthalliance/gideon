Associated infections:
- 6.5% of HIV-positive patients have tuberculosis (Cali, 1991 to 1992). 3
   11.9% of tuberculosis patients in Bogota are HIV-positive. (2001). 4
- 38.6% of patients with HIV infection in Medellin are HBV-positive, and 0.8% are HCV-positive 5
- 10.4% of HIV-positive patients in Bogota are infested by Cryptosporidium, 25.2% Blastocystis hominis, 3.5% Microsporidium and 13% Entamoeba histolytica (2001). 6
   3.9% of HIV-positive patients in Medellin are infested by Microsporidium (2001 to 2002). 7
- 931 cases of cryptococcosis were reported in Colombia over 9 years in a national survey (240 cases per 100, 000 per year; 300 per 100, 000 per year among AIDS patients).  78.1% were HIV-positive and 82.7% were male. (1997 to 2005) 8
- Two cases (1995 and 1997) of superinfection by paracoccidioidomycosis in AIDS patients have been reported. 9

Notable outbreaks:
     1993 - An outbreak (13 cases) of HIV infection was reported at a dialysis center. 10
