Demography and risk factors:
- Cases reported to December 1997:  85% males; 93% ages 15 to 49; 61% heterosexual; 22% men who have sex with men; 4% IDU; 9% transfusion or hemophilia-related; 4% mother to infant.
- Cases during 1997 to 1999: 90% ages 15 to 49; 87% males; 58% heterosexual; 19% men who have sex with men; 9% IDU; 9% transfusion/hemophilia; 5% mother to infant.
- Cases during 1997 to 2001: 89% ages 15 to 49; 88% males; 56% heterosexual; 32% men who have sex with men; 2% IDU; 8% transfusion/hemophilia; 1% mother to infant.

Seven new cases were reported in 1995.
