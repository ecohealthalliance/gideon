GIDEON - Epidemiology: Ebola in Sudan
Ebola in Sudan

An endemic focus is present in the southern region (Juba, Maridi - 1988 publication) 1

Notable outbreaks:
     1976 - An outbreak (284 cases, 151 fatal) was reported in Nzara and Maridi.  These were the world's first cases of Ebola. 2 3 4
     1979 - An outbreak (34 cases, 22 fatal) was reported in Nzara.
     2004 - An outbreak (17 cases, 7 fatal) was reported in Yambio, Western Equatoria (southern region). 5 6 7
